Introduction. Play Dominoes game

There are 3 versions of online domino games: Five-Up (aka Muggins or All Fives) as well as Draw and Block. All-Fives is a scoring dominoes game, there you need to score tailing points, Draw and Block are the classical dominoes games of the blocking type.

The object of Dominoes is to be the first person to attain the agreed-upon number of points (100 - 500). Points may be awarded during the play of the hand by making the exposed ends of the chain total to a multiple of five (in Five-Up). The winner at the end of each hand also scores points for all the pips remaining in the other player's hand. Players can also select between 7-bone hand and 9-bone hand. There are many dominoes game options like using or not using spinner etc.

Here you can play a two-players dominoes game thereby preventing any collusion that is rampant in partners dominoes. There are 28 dominos and each player gets 7 (or 9 in a 9-bone variation) dominos at start. You can also learn domino by playing against computer.
