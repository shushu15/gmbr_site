Other games to learn or play online

Web Apps to play alone and to improve your gaming skills or to play online with other people

Cribbage

6-card cribbage is a popular competitive card game for two players where you gain points for combinations in hand and during play.

Gin Rummy

A card game for two players where you complete your hand by changing cards to "meld" as many your cards as possible.

Canasta

A game for two players of Rummy family, with value-only melds and discard pile special rules, played by 2-pack card deck

Spades

A game for 2,3,4 players with trumps and the bidding stage

Backgammon

Worldwide famous game on the special board and with dice. There are short and long version, double cube and other features in backgammon.

Dominoes

A popular tile game with many variations like All-Fives or Muggins, Draw, Block, Train etc. Can be played by the different number of dominoes bones
